Shaping Crossfit


Rich did a lot to shape Crossfit as we know it.

He showed and shows us how to be a leader as a competitor, both in and out of competition.

From running a successful business in Crossfit, to teaching others how to compete, meanwhile raising a family, this man seems unstoppable.

Rich got his big break in the 2010 Crossfit Games as a runner-up.

Then, in 2011 he figured it out. He figured out how to outwork, outperform, and dig deeper than other athlete in history.

2011, 2012, 2013, 14', 15', 16' he found himself standing on the 1st place pedestal of the Crossfit Games.

As a leader in the workout community, he started taking on the team challenges and ended up taking his team on a winning streak from 2015-2021, skipping 2020.

Be sure to check out his Wikipedia entry for a full breakdown on his successes.
